Online Appendix (Dugo Giorgio Pigato 2024)

This document serves as a supplementary appendix to the paper "Multivariate Rough Volatility" (Dugo, Giorgio, Pigato 2024), which is available at [link]. Readers are encouraged to consult the main paper.

Navigation Index

  • 1. Goodness of covariance fit (exact estimation)
    • contains additional evidence of the goodness of fit of our model to the log-volatility data by showing empirical and theoretical cross-covariances with estimates according to Section 5.1.

  • 2. Empirical evidence of asymptotic regime
    • contains plots of the empirical auto and cross-covariances of log-volatility data as functions of suitable powers of the lags (related to the Hurst exponents), which, according to the asymptotic relationship, should be linear.

  • 3. Estimates in the asymptotic regime
    • presents estimates of the model in the small-alpha (slow mean reversion) regime on log-volatility data, where the approximate asymptotic auto and cross-covariances are directly employed as moment conditions in the MDE procedure.

  • 4. Goodness of covariance fit (asymptotic regime)
    • contains additional evidence of the goodness of fit of our model in the asymptotic regime to the log-volatility data by showing empirical and theoretical cross-covariances with estimates in line with Section 5.2.

  • 5. Estimates in the causal regime
    • presents estimates of the model in the causal regime on log-volatility data, where the process only depends on past white noise realizations and eta_ij is constrained to be a function of rho_ij, H_i, and H_j. These are used in the analysis of spillovers (Section 6).

  • 6. Goodness of covariance fit (causal regime)
    • contains evidence of the goodness of fit of our model in the causal regime to the log-volatility data by showing empirical and theoretical cross-covariances with estimates from the previous point.
